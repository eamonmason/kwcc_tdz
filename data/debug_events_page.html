ZwiftPower Results

VI
Variability Index. A calulation of how even your power was. A lower number is more of a TT effort, while higher suggests many sprints with plenty of rest. It is simply your Normalised power / Average power.
NP
An estimate of the power you could achieve if your power had remained constant.
Avg 20m
If strava data is not available this is calculated from your average power using a modifier percentage based on time of effort
Weight
This is the lowest weight you had during the race.

Filtering

Zwift Category Enforcement

The Zwift Category Enforcement system was designed to prevent Zwifters from entering categories below their recommended level. The events are usually signalled in the Game, Companion or Zwift Web with a icon.

Category Enforcement DOES NOT USE the historic ZwiftPower categories (that are solely based on your best 20 minute power), but instead uses your complete power curve data from ALL activities to determine your category.

Check your Suggested category in the Fitness stats section of your Zwift feed or profile page.

Read more about Category Enforcement events in the official FAQ .
